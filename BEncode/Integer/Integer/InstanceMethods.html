- (String) bencode

Encodes an integer into a bencoded integer. Bencoded integers are represented by an ‘i’ followed by the number in base 10 followed by an ‘e’. 

  3.bencode #=> "i3e"
  -3.bencode #=> "i-3e"

  • (String) — the bencoded integer
