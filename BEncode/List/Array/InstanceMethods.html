- (String) bencode

Encodes an array into a bencoded list. Bencoded lists are encoded as an ‘l’ followed by their elements (also bencoded) followed by an ‘e’. 

  [:eggs, "ham", 3, 4.1].bencode #=> "l4:eggs3:hami3ei4ee"

  • (String) — the bencoded list
